## What the Game Can Do Now

### How It Plays

- **Flat or 3D View** — You can play the game in a simple flat view or in a 3D view.
- **Fly the Plane** — Move your mouse to steer your fighter jet around the screen.
- **Shoot** — Click to fire bullets at the targets.
- **Enemies Shoot Back** — The targets fire bullets at you too.
- **Health Bar** — You have a health bar. If enemy bullets hit you, you lose health. When health runs out, the game is over.
- **Score Saving** — You earn points when you hit a target. Your score is saved so it does not disappear when you close the browser.
- **Hits Register** — The game notices when bullets hit targets or when enemy bullets hit you.

### Screens and Menus

- **Welcome Screen** — A start screen with a Start button and a Help button so you can learn the controls before playing.
- **Game Over Screen** — Shows your final score and lets you play again.
- **Help Screen** — A pop-up list showing all the keyboard controls while you play.
- **Pause** — You can pause the game and everything stops moving.

### Controls

| Key or Action | What It Does |
|---|---|
| `Enter` | Start the game |
| `P` | Pause or unpause |
| `H` | Show or hide the help screen |
| `M` | Turn sound on or off |
| Move your mouse | Steer the plane |
| Click | Fire bullets |

### Looks and Sound

- **3D Shapes** — The plane, bullets, clouds, and targets are shown as 3D shapes.
- **Clouds** — Clouds appear in the background while you play.
- **Shadows** — Shadows are drawn to make things look more real.
- **Camera View** — A special camera setup is used for the 3D scene.
- **Music and Sound** — Background music plays, and you can mute it.

## Features We Want to Add

| # | Feature | Category | What It Means |
|---|---------|----------|---------------|
| 1 | **Hold to Shoot** | Gameplay | Keep shooting bullets automatically when you hold the mouse button down, instead of clicking every time. |
| 2 | **Moving Targets** | Gameplay | Make targets fly side to side or in patterns instead of staying still. |
| 3 | **Gets Harder Over Time** | Gameplay | Enemies show up faster and move quicker as your score goes up. |
| 4 | **Power-ups** | Gameplay | Destroying a target may drop helpful items like extra health, a shield, faster shooting, or stronger bullets. |
| 5 | **Boss Enemy** | Gameplay | A big, tough enemy appears every so many points with its own special attacks. |
| 6 | **Extra Lives** | Gameplay | Give the player more than one life instead of ending the game as soon as health hits zero. |
| 7 | **High Score List** | Gameplay | Keep a list of the best scores across all play sessions. |
| 8 | **Levels** | Gameplay | Move up to new levels with different backgrounds, new enemies, and higher difficulty. |
| 9 | **Settings Menu** | Menu | A menu inside the game to change sound volume, difficulty, picture quality, and controls. |
| 10 | **Explosion Effects** | Looks | Add small explosions and spark effects when things get hit. |
| 11 | **3D Sound** | Sound | Sounds like bullets and explosions come from the direction they happen on screen. |
| 12 | **Ground or Ocean Below** | Looks | Add land or water underneath the flying area. |
| 13 | **Better Weapons** | Gameplay | Let the player improve their gun over time, like shooting in a spread or through multiple targets. |
| 14 | **Combo Bonus** | Gameplay | Hitting many targets in a row gives extra points. |
| 15 | **Badges** | Gameplay | Unlock little awards for reaching goals, like scoring 1,000 points or surviving for one minute. |
| 16 | **Fill the Screen** | Menu | Let the player make the game take up the whole screen. |
| 17 | **Phone and Tablet Support** | Controls | Add touch controls so you can play on mobile devices by dragging to move and tapping to shoot. |
| 18 | **Save and Continue Later** | Gameplay | Let the player close the browser and come back to the same game later. |
| 19 | **Different Enemy Types** | Gameplay | Add more kinds of enemies, like fast scouts, heavy tanks, and zig-zagging flyers. |
| 20 | **Radar** | Menu | Show a small map on screen so you can see where enemies are. |

## What to Work on First

These are the features that would improve the game the most with the least effort:

1. **Hold to Shoot** — Easy to add and makes shooting feel much better.
2. **Moving Targets** — Makes the game feel more alive and exciting.
3. **Power-ups** — Makes players want to play again and again.
4. **Settings Menu** — Lets players adjust the game to their liking.
5. **Explosion Effects** — Makes hits feel more satisfying to watch.
