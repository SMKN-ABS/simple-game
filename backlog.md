## Hold to Shoot
Keep shooting bullets automatically when you hold the mouse button down, instead of clicking every time.

## Gets Harder Over Time
Enemies show up faster and move quicker as your score goes up or as time passes.

## Expand Power-ups
Add extra health, shield, and faster shooting drops beyond the existing `doubleBullet`.

## Extra Lives
Give the player more than one life instead of ending the game as soon as health hits zero.

## High Score List
Keep a list of the best scores across all play sessions.

## Settings Menu
A menu inside the game to change sound volume, difficulty, picture quality, and controls.

## Explosion Effects
Add small explosions and spark effects when things get hit.

## Different Enemy Types
Add more kinds of enemies, like fast scouts, heavy tanks, and zig-zagging flyers.

## Better Weapons
Let the player improve their gun over time, like shooting in a spread or through multiple targets.

## Badges / Achievements
Unlock little awards for reaching goals, like scoring 1,000 points or surviving for one minute.

## Radar / Mini Map
Show a small map on screen so you can see where enemies are.

## Boss Enemy
A big, tough enemy appears every so many points with its own special attacks.

## Levels
Move up to new levels with different backgrounds, new enemies, and higher difficulty.

## Ground or Ocean Below
Add land or water underneath the flying area for visual immersion.

## Phone and Tablet Support
Add touch controls so you can play on mobile devices by dragging to move and tapping to shoot.

## Save and Continue Later
Let the player close the browser and come back to the same game later.

## Fill the Screen
Let the player make the game take up the whole screen.

## 3D Positional Sound
Sounds like bullets and explosions come from the direction they happen on screen.
